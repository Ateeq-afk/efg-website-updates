# Competitor Analysis — Events First Group

**Date:** March 2026  
**Purpose:** SEO & market positioning analysis for eventsfirstgroup.com

---

## Executive Summary

EFG operates in the **executive technology summit** space in MENA. Competitors fall into two categories:

1. **Large Expos** — GISEC, GITEX (10,000+ attendees, exhibition-focused)
2. **Executive Summits** — Evanta, Pulse, IDC, 3novex (100-500 attendees, peer-driven)

EFG's positioning is closer to Category 2 but with a unique **practitioner-first, no-sales-pitch** approach.

---

## Tier 1: Large Expo Competitors

### 1. GISEC Global
- **URL:** gisec.ae
- **Format:** Massive expo + conference (25,000+ attendees, 180 countries)
- **Dates:** May 5-7, 2026
- **Venue:** Dubai Exhibition Centre, Expo City
- **Organizer:** Dubai World Trade Centre
- **Focus:** Cybersecurity (broad — startups to enterprise)

**Strengths:**
- Largest cybersecurity event in MEA
- Government backing (DESC partnership)
- Multiple specialized stages (Dark Stage, X-Labs, Gov Stage, OT Stage)
- Strong brand recognition

**Weaknesses:**
- Overwhelming scale — hard to make meaningful connections
- Heavy vendor presence (sales-focused)
- Generic content for mass audience

**SEO Notes:**
- Ranks #1 for "cybersecurity conference Dubai"
- Strong domain authority from government backlinks
- Title: "GISEC GLOBAL | The World's Leading Cyber Security..."

## EFG Differentiators

✅ **Practitioner-first content** — No sales pitches, real case studies  
✅ **Intimate formats** — 15-50 person boardrooms, not 500+ conferences  
✅ **Multi-series coverage** — Cyber, OT Security, OPEX, Data/AI  
✅ **Regional depth** — GCC + Africa + India  
✅ **Quality over quantity** — Curated delegates, not mass attendance
